Copyright is a tool that limits freedom

The Pirate Bay, a famous torrent search engine - by Broblike, CC BY-SA 4.0

In an era where information and culture are more accessible than ever, the effectiveness and fairness of copyright is being questioned. The reality is that copyright limits the sharing of knowledge and cultures, causing harm to all of humanity, especially to those living in poorer countries who do not have access to the desired materials. It is time to reflect on how this regulation, originally created to protect the author, is actually crystallizing works and ideas, thus denying many the opportunity to enjoy and contribute to a shared cultural heritage.
